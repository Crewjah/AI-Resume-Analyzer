About This Tool

How It Works

Our AI Resume Analyzer uses advanced algorithms to evaluate your resume across multiple dimensions:

  • Content Analysis: Evaluates word count, use of action verbs, and quantified achievements
  • Keyword Optimization: Identifies technical and soft skills mentioned in your resume
  • ATS Compatibility: Checks formatting and structure for Applicant Tracking Systems
  • Structure Evaluation: Ensures all essential resume sections are present and well-organized
  • Completeness Check: Verifies that required information like contact details is included

Honest Analysis

This tool provides genuine, actionable feedback based on industry best practices. All scores are calculated using transparent algorithms - no inflated numbers or fake results.

Privacy

Your resume data is processed securely and is not stored. Analysis happens in real-time, and your data is immediately discarded after processing.
